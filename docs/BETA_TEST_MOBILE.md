# ButterGolf Mobile App - Beta Testing Guide

Thanks for helping test ButterGolf on mobile! 🏌️ Your feedback is invaluable in making this the best golf club marketplace it can be.

**Download:** Available via TestFlight (iOS) or Play Store Beta (Android)

---

## What I Need From You

Spend 10-15 minutes trying out the app. Don't worry about breaking anything – that's what testing is for! Just use it like you would any shopping app and let me know what you think.

---

## 1. Sign Up & Sign In

**Try these:**

- Create a new account (tap "Sign Up")
- Sign out and sign back in
- Try the "Forgot Password" flow if you want

**Tell me:**

- Was anything confusing?
- Did the keyboard behave normally when typing?
- Did buttons respond when you tapped them?

---

## 2. Browsing & Buying

**Try these:**

- Scroll through the home screen
- Tap on a category to browse clubs
- Open a product to see its details
- Swipe through the product photos
- Tap the heart to favourite something
- Try "Buy Now" or "Make Offer"

**Tell me:**

- Did scrolling feel smooth?
- Were buttons easy to tap? (not too small?)
- Could you see the photos clearly?
- Did the condition ratings make sense?
- Did anything feel slow or freeze?

---

## 3. Selling a Club

**Try these:**

- Tap "Sell" in the bottom navigation
- Complete the seller setup (verify your identity)
- Try creating a listing with photos from your camera roll
- Use the condition sliders

**Tell me:**

- Was the seller sign-up clear?
- Did taking/uploading photos work properly?
- Were the form fields easy to fill in?
- Did the keyboard cover anything important?

---

## 4. Your Account & Navigation

**Try these:**

- Tap through the bottom navigation tabs
- Go to your Account/Profile
- Check your orders and favourites
- Pull down to refresh on any list screen

**Tell me:**

- Was it obvious how to get around the app?
- Did the back button always work as expected?
- Could you find what you needed?
- Did anything feel different from other apps you use?

---

## What Kind of Feedback Helps

✅ **Super helpful:**

- "I tapped X but nothing happened"
- "The app crashed when I did Y"
- "I didn't understand what Z meant"
- "This button was too small to tap easily"
- "The keyboard covered the input field"
- "I expected swiping to do X but it did Y"

✅ **Also helpful:**

- "The colours looked washed out"
- "Text was hard to read"
- "Photos took ages to load"
- "I liked how X worked!"
- "This felt different from how other apps work"

---

## How to Send Feedback

**Email me at:** josh@rwxt.org

Please include:

- What you were trying to do
- What happened (or didn't happen)
- A screenshot if you can (especially for visual issues or errors)
- Your phone model (e.g., iPhone 14, Samsung S23)
- iOS or Android version if you know it

**Tip:** On iPhone, press Side + Volume Up to screenshot. On Android, press Power + Volume Down.

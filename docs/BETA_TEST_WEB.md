# ButterGolf Web App - Beta Testing Guide

Thanks for helping test ButterGolf! 🏌️ Your feedback is invaluable in making this the best golf club marketplace it can be.

**Website:** [buttergolf.com](https://buttergolf.com)

---

## What I Need From You

Spend 10-15 minutes trying out the app. Don't worry about breaking anything – that's what testing is for! Just use it like you would any shopping website and let me know what you think.

---

## 1. Sign Up & Sign In

**Try these:**

- Create a new account (use a real email you can access)
- Sign out and sign back in
- Try "Forgot Password" if you want

**Tell me:**

- Was anything confusing?
- Did you receive the verification email quickly?
- Did logging in feel smooth?

---

## 2. Browsing & Buying

**Try these:**

- Browse the homepage and click through some categories
- Use the filters (brand, condition, price)
- Open a product to see its details
- Add something to your favourites (heart icon)
- Click "Buy Now" or "Make Offer" on a listing

**Tell me:**

- Could you find what you were looking for?
- Were the product photos and descriptions helpful?
- Did the condition ratings make sense?
- Was anything slow or glitchy?

---

## 3. Selling a Club

**Try these:**

- Click "Sell" in the navigation
- Complete the seller setup (you'll need to verify your identity – use test details if you prefer)
- Try creating a listing with photos
- Drag the condition sliders and see how they work

**Tell me:**

- Was the seller sign-up process clear?
- Did the listing form make sense?
- Was uploading photos easy?
- Did you understand what each field was asking for?

---

## 4. Your Account

**Try these:**

- Go to your Account/Profile area
- Check your orders (if you made any)
- Look at your favourites/saved items
- Explore the seller dashboard (if you listed something)

**Tell me:**

- Could you find what you needed?
- Was the layout easy to navigate?
- Anything missing that you expected to see?

---

## What Kind of Feedback Helps

✅ **Super helpful:**

- "I clicked X but nothing happened"
- "I didn't understand what Y meant"
- "The page loaded slowly when I did Z"
- "I expected to see X but saw Y instead"
- "The text was hard to read on the blue background"
- "This button was too small / hard to find"

✅ **Also helpful:**

- "The colours feel too bright/dull"
- "The font looks different on this page"
- "This photo looked blurry"
- "I liked how X worked!"

---
